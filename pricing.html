Validating Open Banking Data Is a Moving and Expensive Target

Open banking data changes in ways you can’t reliably test. Changes to the data model can be introduced by aggregators or by the originating institutions themselves. This uncertainty leads to incorrect data, increased service tickets, and an ongoing maintenance burden on engineering teams.

MapleSchema decouples your application code from these changes by enforcing a stable, versioned transaction schema. Engineering teams can focus on solving business problems and delivering value instead of maintaining mappers, coordinating with service teams, and chasing upstream data issues.
